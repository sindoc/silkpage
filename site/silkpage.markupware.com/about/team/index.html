Sina

Sina K. Heshmati

Sina enjoys dabbling with just about anything related to Markup and Semantic Web technologies. He began working with computers in 1996 when he was knee-high to a grasshopper. Unknowingly dubbed SinDoc by his brother, most of his friends and family members agree that he spends far too much time in front of a computer screen learning as much about Web as possible. Javad

Javad K. Heshmati

Javad began working with computers in the late 80's. He received his B.S. degree in Computer Science from Vesalius College and his M.S. degree in software engineering from the V.U.B. university. He has been involved in object-oriented design and development his entire career, and has been coding Java since its early days. Currently, he is a Java architect with Dixite in Brussels, Belgium, where he has designed and implemented Java and XML based solutions for customers around the world. For MarkupWare, Javad provides XML consultancy.
