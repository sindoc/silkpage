Participate

SilkPage is an open source project. That means it is primarily community-driven work. You too, can participate. Here are some of the ways you can participate/contribute to the SilkPage project.

  • Report Bugs: Bug reports are very helpful to us and great way to contribute to the SilkPage project. Please make sure you can reproduce the problem, and be very explicit in your description. Vist the developer Page to report bugs.

  • Contribute Code: sources are always packed in the distributions. If there's a feature you'd like to see, or a bug you'd like to see fixed, you have the power to make that happen. Vist the developer Page to browse sources.

  • Design our logo: that's right we need a well designed logo. Your design will be displayed on every page that is SilkPaged. Please refer to the Logo Design Guidelines to find about the requirements.

Willing to help? Get in touch.
