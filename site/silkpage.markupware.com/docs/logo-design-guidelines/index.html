Logo Design Guidelines

Design our logo. SilkPage logo is meant to be displayed on every page that is SilkPaged. As such the logo must be a quality logo. The following criteria should be taken into account when designing the logo:

  • Identity: demonstrate Silk. Silk is meant to indicate fine quality. For example, one could use a butterfly to identify silk.

  • Text: work out the word SilkPage in the logo.

  • Color: it's preferred that the logo's color(s) match well with the SilkPage's web site colors.

  • Shape: it should be suitable to be displayed in the footer of web pages that are SilkPaged.

  • Size: at least two sizes are required:

    • Average: to be displayed on top of SilkPage web site pages.

    • Small: to be displayed in the footer of every page that is silkpaged.
