SilkPaged

A page is considered to be SilkPaged if it's generated by the SilkPage Web Publishing Framework. SilkPage output conforms with requirements outlined in SilkPage Design Goals .

We are happy to grant you the right to use the SilkPaged Logo on the pages that passed through the SilkPage Publishing Framework.

SilkPaged Icons and Logos

SilkPage has no logo, it's yet to be designed, maybe by you. Your design will be displayed on every page that is SilkPaged. Please refer to the Logo Design Guidelines to find about the logo requirements.

No request permission is needed for the SilkPaged logos. However, SilkPaged logos usage are subject to the following terms:

  • The icons must not be used in any manner in which implies SilkPage or MarkupWare sponsorship of your product.

  • The icon must be an active link to http://silkpage.markupware.com/docs/silkpaged/
