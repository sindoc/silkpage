Roadmap

This page outlines the roadmap for the upcoming SilkPage releases in an attempt to define what the SilkPage project is trying to achieve, and how we plan to achieve it.

Please bear in mind that as with any open source project, all decisions are made by the community at large - that also includes you. If you see something here you disagree with, or think needs further discussion, then lets us know.

Release Types

Stable Builds

are builds that have been found to be stable enough for most people to use. They are promoted to become releases after they have been tested and used for a few weeks and deemed reasonably stable.

Releases

are builds that have been declared major releases by the SilkPage development team.
