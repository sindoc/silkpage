# Trademarks

The SilkPage name, logos, and related branding may be protected separately from
copyright licensing.

Unless and until a separate trademark policy is published:

- no trademark license is granted by the GPL;
- no trademark license is granted by contributor agreements; and
- commercial customers should not assume branding rights beyond nominative use.
